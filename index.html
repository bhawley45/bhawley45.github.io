About Me

Hello there! I'm Brandon Hawley, a father of four, expert software architect, aspiring scientist, artist, and game enthusiast. I believe creativity and imagination are some of our greatest gifts as humans; my passion in life derives from this very idea. I dream to further human development, improve the human condition, write narratives and create works of art that inspire humanity.

Through my time in the tech industry I've become adept at many different languages and frameworks including: C++, C#, Java, Python, and more including Unreal and Unity engines. I have hobbies in astrophysics, sound engineering, and writing. I also have experience in running a computer repair buisness for 2 years, and a YouTube side-buisness for 1. I love a challenge, learning new things, and making people laugh.
