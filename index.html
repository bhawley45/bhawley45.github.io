My story so far...

  Hello, I'm Brandon Hawley, a dedicated father and game developer with six years of software development experience. As a kid, I was inspired by gaming; my imagination would run wild with fantastic stories and worlds. I filled journals with sketches, references, and writings until their bindings fell apart. But as time went on, I lost faith that my dreams were possible. I started at MCC pursuing an associate's in Electrical Engineering, hoping for financial stability. I quickly realized it was not a good fit.

  I switched to Computer Science and started fresh at Missouri Western. However, as COVID-19 started disrupting everything, I realized I was essentially paying for self-education. I decided it was time to rethink my approach. I got married and founded Hawleywood Electronics LLC to support my family, teaching myself software development on the side. Life was good, albeit it hectic - until it wasn't. As the electronics business grew, it began to consume all my time and energy, leaving nothing left for my family or my dreams.

  Then my world suddenly shattered... We lost our 9-month-old son in December of 2020. Losing him was brought indescribable pain. My commitment to Hawleywood Electronics had stolen time from my family that I could never get back, spent on something I didn't even enjoy. I slowly closed my business and entered a season of soul-searching.

  The arrival of our second son brought hope and renewed purpose. It was then that I fully embraced my childhood dream of creating games, resolving no longer to sideline the passions that had ignited my imagination. Life is too short not to pursue your dreams!

  Now, I balance being a father with a renewed passion for game development. This shift has brought me not only fulfillment but invaluable time with my family. Each project is more than just a game—it's a part of my personal revival and a testament to the resilience needed to believe and pursue one's dreams amid life's unpredictability.
